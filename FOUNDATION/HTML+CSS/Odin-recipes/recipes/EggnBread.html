Egg and Bread

Recipe by: Me

Description

A quick and easy Breakfast for those in a hurry
or those too lazy to break out the big guns.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Nutella

Instructions

  1. Cook your eggs however you prefer
  2. (optional) Toast your bread
  3. Spread Nutella onto some of the bread slices
  4. Eat
